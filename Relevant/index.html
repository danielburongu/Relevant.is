Mission Statement

Relevant Exists to Better People's lives, the Planet and Ourselves. By joyfully working together to create a world that works for all.
We’re on a mission to support businesses that matter! We believe that social and environmental problems should never be treated as afterthoughts, but rather prioritized. Our goal is to empower those at the periphery and help them create a better world for everyone.
